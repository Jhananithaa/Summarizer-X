FAQ'S

Got questions? We've got answers! Here are some common inquiries about SummarizerX to help you understand how our tool works and how it can benefit you.

  1. What is SummarizerX?
    The website offers text summarization, rephrasing, text-to-speech, customization, and chatbot tools.
  2. How does summarization work?
    Upload your content, choose a summary length, and our algorithms generate a concise summary.
  3. Is the tool accessible for users with disabilities?
    Yes, it includes Text-to-Speech and is compatible with screen readers for enhanced accessibility.
  4. Can I customize my summary output?
    Absolutely! You can adjust text size, colors, and TTS settings to suit your preferences.
  5. What formats can I export my summaries in?
    You can export summaries in PDF, Word, or text formats for easy sharing and saving.
